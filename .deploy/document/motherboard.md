## JINGYUE X99 TI D4 PLUS

Configuration instructions for the `JINGYUE X99-TI D4 PLUS` motherboard

### BIOS Configuration

- Power on the computer and continuously press `Delete` to enter `BIOS`

- Find `Above 4G Decoding` in `PCI Subsystem Settings` of `Advanced`

- Set `Above 4G Decoding` to `Enabled`

- Press `F10` to save and restart the device
